The award

Stanford Center on Longevity, 2026.

Neevel placed 2nd at the Stanford Center on Longevity Design Challenge — an international student competition hosted annually at the Stanford Longevity Center, focused on design that improves long life.

2nd

International finalists

The Design Challenge selects student teams whose work addresses challenges of aging through design thinking. Finalists are invited to Stanford in April to present to a panel of judges drawn from longevity research, design, and medicine. Neevel was presented on April 14, 2026.

Team

Neevel is designed and built by two students.

Verônica Vanti
Industrial Design · Product
Industrial design student at Feevale University in Brazil. Design lead on Neevel, responsible for the visual and material direction of the system — from the insole form to the nightlight hub.
Andrei Vince
Computer Engineering · Systems
Computer engineering student at UNC Charlotte. Leads the sensing, firmware, and systems work behind Neevel — from three-point pressure telemetry to the coaching loop.
